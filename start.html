First, let's narrow down what you need to do.

What edition of Minecraft are you playing on?

How can you tell what edition you're using?

If you're on Xbox One/Series, PS4/5, or Switch, choose "Bedrock Edition ( )".

If you're on iOS or Android, choose "Bedrock Edition ( / )".

If you're on PC/Mac and see the "Marketplace" button, also choose "Bedrock Edition ( / )".

Otherwise, you should choose "Java Edition ( )".

If you're playing on Minecraft: [Console] Edition, you will not be able to join.
